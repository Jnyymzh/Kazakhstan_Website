DISCOVER THE DIVERS LANDSCAPE OF KAZAKHSTAN

Kazakhstan's geography is a stunning blend of vast steppes, rugged mountains, expansive deserts, and lush forests. As the world's largest landlocked country, it boasts a rich variety of natural wonders:

Kazakhstan's vast steppes cover much of the country, providing open plains ideal for grazing and agriculture.

Official and Commonly Spoken Languages

Kazakhstan is a multilingual country where Kazakh and Russian are the most widely spoken languages.

Languages Spoken in Kazakhstan

Kazakh

The official language of Kazakhstan, spoken by the majority of the population.

Russian

A widely spoken language used in government, business, and daily communication.

Uzbek

Spoken by the Uzbek minority in Kazakhstan, particularly in southern regions.

Ukrainian

Used by ethnic Ukrainians living in Kazakhstan, contributing to the country's linguistic diversity.

Tatar

Spoken by the Tatar community, primarily in urban areas.

German

Once widely spoken by Kazakhstan's ethnic Germans, though now less common.
